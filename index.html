About Alomah's Place & Nature Farm
Alomah's Place & Nature Farm is a 2-hectare organic lettuce farm in Dahilayan, Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon. Producing 100–200 kg of fresh organic lettuce weekly from a 500 sqm growing area, the farm supplies restaurants, hotels, and Shakey's branches across Cagayan de Oro and Iligan City. Four variants are grown: Romaine, Green Leaf, Red Leaf, and Iceberg — all 100% organic, from soil to table.

"From Soil to Table — Organically Grown in the Highlands of Bukidnon"
